ABOUT US

Kenya is the third largest exporter of cut flowers in the world. It boasts of over 200 flower farms across the country; the product range being extensive. Papillon Horticultural Export Company (PHEC) makes its mark by consolidating high quality products from some of the best flower farms across the country; standing out in vast colours, stem lengths and varieties.

We have a well-structured organisation that coordinates the orders; sources for the right quality and quantity that meets the demand; monitors deliveries; does proper documentation as well as shipment follow up.

A cold chain with the right temperature is maintained from the time of harvest up until the product arrives in the hands of the client to maintain fresh produce.

We link up clients to reputable local and international shipping agents and based on agreement terms with the client coordinate the necessary logistical functions.
